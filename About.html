Welcome to Creative Editing Zone

Most companies come into existence because one or more people have a good idea! Creative Editing Zone is no exception. Creative Editing Zone commenced graphics production in 2018 in cooperation with a local company in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh.

The subsidiary in Dhaka is 100% owned and controlled by Creative Editing Zone. It has been our focus from the very beginning that all processes should be internally managed. We have built up the production process, we maintain it, and we are refining it.

Millions of designers and artists look for design inspiration and jobs on Designhill. We encourage creative talent around the world to get hired for design projects, showcase their portfolio and become a part of a global design community -No matter the skill or experience.
